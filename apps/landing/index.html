The Problem

CBAM is getting mandatory.
The tooling isn't ready.

📋

Manual & fragmented

Importers still collect emissions data via email, PDFs, and spreadsheets — scattered across systems that can't be verified.

🔓

No shared trust

Sellers, importers, logistics partners, and auditors each hold partial information with no tamper-resistant shared record.

⏳

Slow and costly

Without automation, compliance teams discover data gaps too late — increasing reporting errors and regulatory risk.

The Solution

Three parties attest. One AI calculates.

1
🏭

Seller declares emissions

The exporter submits production intensity, facility reference, and supporting evidence. Hashed and attested on Solana.

→
2
🏢

Importer submits trade data

Quantity, invoice, product type, and purchase reference — separately recorded on-chain as an independent attestation.

→
3
🚢

Logistics confirms shipment

A third-party logistics partner verifies quantity, origin, and route consistency. Their signature triggers the AI.

→
4
🤖

AI validates and reports

AI agents cross-check all three inputs, compute CBAM exposure, and generate a professional compliance report.

Technology

Built on real infrastructure.

◎

Solana

Multi-party workflow attestation. Immutable, fast, and cheap enough to put every compliance event on-chain.

✦

Gemini AI

Event-driven AI agents validate, calculate, and narrate — turning raw attestation data into audit-ready reports.

⚡

Supabase

Off-chain data, document storage, and real-time dashboard powered by Postgres and a managed cloud stack.
